01 Introduction

At Cartify, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it when you use our website and services.

By using Cartify, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our website.

02 Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with a smoother shopping experience:

  • Account details — name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Shipping & billing information — address and payment details required to fulfil orders.
  • Order history — items purchased, order value, and delivery status.
  • Device & usage data — browser type, IP address, and pages visited.
  • Communications — messages you send to our support team.

03 How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and support responses.
  • To personalise product recommendations and offers.
  • To improve our website, products, and customer service.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorised activity.

04 Cookies

Cartify uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember items in your cart, and understand how customers use our site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

05 Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with:

  • Delivery and logistics partners, to fulfil your orders.
  • Payment processors, to complete secure transactions.
  • Service providers who help us run our website and marketing.
  • Authorities, where required by law.

06 Data Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. While no system is completely infallible, we work continuously to keep your data safe.

07 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
To exercise any of these rights, reach out to us using the contact details at the bottom of this page.

08 Children's Privacy

Cartify is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
